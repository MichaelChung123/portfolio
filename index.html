WORK

KrispyFringe Vintage

Krispyfringe vintage approached me in need of a site redesign. Their old wordpress site had been thrown together years ago. It was essentially emtpy except for a few several year old blogs posts. As a small vintage clothing shop in Chicago, they needed a site that accurately represented their company. Selling a unique blend of vintage goods, they wanted the site to stand on its own and showcase their inventory. Content management by the staff was a must, as was a dependable blogging platform. Etsy was also a huge part of their business, so the site needed to represent that in some form.

The end result was a fully responsive, custom wordpress theme. I customized an existing etsy plugin to fit the responsive site that automatically lists new products as they posted them.
